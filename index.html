About Me

I'm Sam, a Software Engineering student based in Kenya.

  • Name: Samuel Omondi
  • Age: 22
  • Occupation: Web Developer

I'm deeply passionate about working with and supporting young people and giving them a jump-start in their careers. This stems from my own personal experience where I had minimal guidance in my career path. Having gone through that experience of figuring out what I wanted to do with my life, I realized how that could slow anyone down. Especially when someone has a lot of potential and much ability. I want to create an environment that fosters their ability and wants to help them grow. It's vital to have programs like these that take away all the barriers and constraints that prevent people from finding out what they need to be in an industry of their choosing and be successful in any role they pursue.
